Welcome to Coquere

This is Coquere, a research project on cuisines as complex systems by Vaidehi Venkatesan and Dr Ali Minai

Our Data Sources

We crawled EatYourBooks, BBC Good Food, All Recipes and Yummly for collecting a good data store of recipes from varied sources.

The Work Behind

Four years of work and repeated re-works on this project involves a lot of web crawling, NLP and ML in different programming languages from C# to Python.

Results

Culinary Network Analysis of world cuisines and recipe recommendation system are key research results of our project.
